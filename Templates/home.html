Flower Classifier

Welcome to the Flower Classifier app. Upload an image of a flower to the system. The system will analyze the image using machine learning algorithms and predict the flower species. Information about the predicted flower species will be displayed, including additional information about the flower and the accuracy of the prediction.







Instructions:
Click on the "Upload" button to select an image of a flower.
Once you have selected the image, click on the "Submit" button to upload it to the system.
The system will process the image and display the predicted flower species along with additional information about the flower and the accuracy of the prediction.

Acceptable Filetypes:
The system accepts the following image file types: PNG, JPG, and JPEG.

Sharing Results:
Sharing your results helps us with conservation efforts to help wildflowers and tracking their progression.

Note:
For best results, please ensure that the image is of a flower and is clear and focused.
